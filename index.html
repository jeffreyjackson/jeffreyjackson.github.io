Previous Work

I've been building software since 2001. I started building Mac apps back in 2005 when Apple went Intel, and a few years later I got on board with the iOS SDK when it first came out. In 2009, I started AutoLean, Inc., a small software company that provides iOS/Mac, Android, and .NET development services for manufacturers and industrial oriented businesses.

Around late 2012 I started volunteering to help get Over off the ground. In 2013 I took a hiatus from everything else to work full time at Over as Founder and Engineer. During this time, we built new rendering engines, a store backend, and a few other popular apps like Obaby & Obaby+, and Overgram. We were also able to team up with Wild Measure to build and submit Cape Town Love in less than 24 hours. Louisville Love was another cool collaborative project we did with the very talented Forest Giant.

Some other side projects I've been involved in include: Menu & Hours, and JumpSeat. In the coming months I plan on starting a new business for providing software products and development services outside of my manufacturing client base.
